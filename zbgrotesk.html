ZB Grotesk Typeface Family

When I started putting this website together I quickly realized that I needed a workhorse grotesque, and i thought this was the perfect excuse for me to start working on my own take on this. The typeface should be subtle enough to work in long pieces of text, with a nod to early grostesque faces, but still have a contemporary and almost naive-ish rude character. The family also work as a variable font and will soon be updated with some funky alternates and a negative-contrast version to make it even more versatile and playfull.
