Zukunft Zukunft

Poster and visual work for the graphic design course exhibition at Kunsthøjskolen Holbæk, in the fall of 2017.

The students were to make up their own future scenario and create a graphic object that in some way communicated that scenario. The students were encouraged to break away from graphic design in its tradional sense, and to use whatever medium and format they thought relevant in the communication of their individual scenario.
