Shapes & States

Shapes & States is the results of a composition exercise done with the students at Kunsthøjskolen Holbæk from 2016 - 2018.
The exercise takes a look at the relation between certain states of mind and abstract shape and how these differ.
The students were to visually communicate the words - fragile, tired, open, angry, drunk, heavy using only black and white cardboard. The shapes should be purely abstract form, and not resemble faces, objects, signs or spell out words.
